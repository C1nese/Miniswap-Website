Private Placement

The total amount of MINI is 1 billion of which 15% will be issued in the form of private placement. Before the product launched, the ETH/MINI private equity pool will be opened first. The user redeems a certain amount of ETH into the fund pool to obtain the redeemed MINI.

The fund pool uses a ladder exchange algorithm: In the first round of private placement, 1ETH can be exchanged for 100901MINI with a total of 2018039 pieces.

In the second round of private placement, 1ETH can be exchanged for 93838MINI with a total of 2815164 pieces. In the tenth round of private placement, 1ETH can be exchanged for 52510MINI with a total of 5776154 pieces. And so on...

Participation method- The private equity pool system is developed with a smart contract. When a user enters ETH into Miniswap, the smart contract will return the corresponding amount of MINI to the user's transfer wallet according to the current exchange ratio. [Please be sure to use your own Ethereum wallet to transfer coins, do not transfer coins through the exchange withdrawal method]
